Our Story

Founded in 2001, Brewlicious Café began with a simple yet passionate mission: to serve the finest coffee and create a warm, welcoming atmosphere for all. What started as a small coffee shop in the heart of New York City has grown into a beloved brand with 12 locations spread across the United States. From the bustling streets of San Francisco to the serene suburbs of Austin, each Brewlicious Café embodies our commitment to quality and community.

At Brewlicious Café, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage—it's an experience. Our expert baristas craft each cup with care, using only the highest quality beans sourced from sustainable farms around the world. Whether you're in the mood for a classic espresso or an adventurous seasonal blend, our menu offers something for every palate.

Our headquarters, located in New York City, is the heart of our operations. It's here that our team of passionate coffee enthusiasts works tirelessly to innovate and improve, ensuring that every cup of Brewlicious coffee meets our high standards. We are proud to be a part of the vibrant New York community and take great pride in our roots, which influence our unique blend of urban sophistication and friendly service.

Over the years, Brewlicious Café has become more than just a place to grab a coffee; it's a gathering spot for friends, a workspace for freelancers, and a cozy nook for book lovers. Our inviting interiors, designed with a blend of modern and rustic elements, provide the perfect backdrop for any occasion.

Join us at one of our 12 locations across the USA and discover why Brewlicious Café is synonymous with exceptional coffee and warm hospitality. Whether you're a lifelong coffee lover or a curious newcomer, we're here to make your coffee experience extraordinary. Welcome to Brewlicious Café—where every cup tells a story.

Our Mission

Our commitment to our loyal customers and to excellence extends beyond our coffee. We are dedicated to sustainable practices, from sourcing eco-friendly materials for our cups and packaging to supporting local charities and community initiatives. At Brewlicious Café, we believe in giving back to the communities that support us.

We truly believe in giving back to the community and we always try our best to give the best care to our valuable customers, support our community and give upmost respect to our hardworking and dedicated staff.
